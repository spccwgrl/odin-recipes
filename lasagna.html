Lasagna

Description

It's a type of pasta made of wavy sheets, layered in meat, tomato sauce, and cheese.

Originated in Italy during the Middle Ages and traditionally ascribed to the city of Naples.

  • lasagna pasta sheet
  • marinara
  • parmesan
  • ground beef
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the wavy pasta sheets.
  2. Layer the lasagna sheets with filling and assort into a tray.
  3. Bake lasagna.
  4. Set oven to bake for 400 degrees fahrenheit for 30 minutes.
  5. Eat.
